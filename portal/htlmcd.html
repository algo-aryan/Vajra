General Guidelines

  • Health Trade License issues by Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD)- MCD will issue Health Trade License to the trade under policy decided by the Public Health Department to
  • Necessity of Municipal Licenses As per the section 417 of DMC ACT 1957(amended time to time) provides that any person whosoever desires to do trade under public health policy, is required to obtain license under Health Trade.
  • Licensees need to fulfil all the terms and condition as mentioned in Public Health Policy.
  • Applicant must ensure the availability of all documents and certificates prior to apply for the Health trade License.
  • The applicant/ Licensee cannot claim demand immunity from action or penalties on the ground that the Health Trade License has been granted without prior verification.

About Us

Health Trade License issues by Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD)- MCD will issue Health Trade License to the trade under policy decided by the Public Health Department.

MCD- Health Trade License Delhi is the license related to the trading, manufacturing, storage, restaurant and food supply activities which is mandatory for the businesses to take as per Section 2 (59) of Delhi Municipal Corporation Act (DMC), 1957.

Necessity of Municipal Licenses- As per the section 417 of DMC ACT 1957(amended time to time) provides that any person whosoever desires to do trade under public health policy, is required to obtain license under Health Trade.
