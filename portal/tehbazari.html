To Know Important Instructions while filling Factory License

  • All industrial unit Applicant must apply for factory license through MCD portal only.
  • Applicants must submit correct details online in application formats.
  • Registration of users/applicants is mandatory for all.
  • Relevant provisions of DPCC, MPD-2021 and directions of Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in the matter of MC Mehta Vs UOI & Ors are necessarily to be complied with alongwith directions of Hon’ble NGT in various matters.
  • Existing Labour Laws, Pollution Control Norms shall be applicable on all industrial units.

General Guidelines

  • “Read (SOP) to understand flow of Factory License
  • “Read User Manual”
  • “MPD - 2021”
  • During scrutiny, if department finds any ambiguity in the application form, the application will be rejected and no refund of any fee will be made.
  • Factory Licensing Department will not be responsible for multiple payments made by any applicant through online payment gateway. The issue shall be liable to be resolved between banker and applicant only.
  • Applicants should download the payment receipts and copy of application for future reference.
  • Penalty, as per provisions, for late renewal shall be applicable after 30th April every year.

To Know Important Instructions while filling Tahbazari Renew Form

  • Tehbazari, a combination of two words i.e., "Teh" + "Bazari", means to utilize surface for the purpose to sell anything.
  • As per the order of Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in year 2002, fresh allotment of Tehbazari sites has been stopped.
  • The last allotment was made in 2002 before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in a PIL titled “Gainda Ram vs MCD & Others”
  • Permit is valid for One Year and valid up to 31st March of the Financial Year.

General Guidelines

  • “Read SOP to understand flow of Renew of Tehbazari License”
  • Tehbazari Permits will be granted for the following Ownership Categories
    • General
    • Widows of Ex-servicemen
    • Widows of MCD Employees
    • Wife of Deceased MCD Employee
    • Handicapped Persons
  • Processes involved in Tehbazari Application
    • Renewal of Tehbazari Permit
    • Sale/Purchase
    • Site Transfer
    • Mutation
    • Deactivation
    • Cancellation
  • For every three years, 10% will be increased on the last annual permit fee.
  • War Widows and PCO Booths of Handicapped Persons are exempted from the increase of the renewal charges.

To Know Important Instructions while filling Hawking Renew Form

  • A hawker is a person who sells things that can be easily moved from place to place. They are also known as peddlers, costermongers, or street vendors.
  • Permit is valid for One Year and valid up to 31st March of the Financial Year.

    • Handicapped Persons
  • Processes involved in Hawking Application

  • Following are Permit Types
    • On Bi-cycle/Tricycle/Rikshaw/Hand Cart – Eatables
    • On B-cycle/Tricycle/Rikshaw/Hand Cart – Non-Eatables
    • On Motor Cycle/Mechanised Tricycle/Auto Rikshaw/Scooter (three and four wheeler) – Eatables
    • On Motor Cycle/Mechanized Tricycle/Auto Rikshaw/Scooter (three and four wheeler) – -Non-Eatables
  • For every three years, 20% will be increased on the last annual permit fee.
  • War Widows and PCO Booths of Handicapped Persons are exempted from the increase of the renewal charges.

To Know Important Instructions while filling Hackney Renew Form

  • Hackney Carriage used to be referred as any wheeled vehicle pulled by animals/ man and used for the conveyance of passengers and to carry goods which is kept, or offered, or plies, for hire.
  • Here in DMCs following types of hackney carriages are referred
    • Cycle Rickshaw
    • Rickshaw Puller
    • Cycle Trolley
  • Note: - In the year 2014, the Supreme Court banned bullock cart races across the country as it violates the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, according to which persons in-charge of animals should not inflict unnecessary pain or suffering upon them. Hence, Hackney Carriage Licenses for Rickshaw and Rickshaw trolley were encouraged.

General Guidelines

  • “Read SOP to understand flow of Fresh & Renew of Hackney License”
  • Processes involved in Hackney Carriage License Application
    • Fresh Application of License
    • Renewal of License
    • Amendment
    • Surrender (As per DMCs, no case of surrender has been dealt so far)
