  • Kindly note down the system generated registration number for future communication.

  • License issues by the MCD can be downloaded / printed.

General Guidelines

  • Health Trade License issues by Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD)- MCD will issue Health Trade License to the trade under policy decided by the Public Health Department to
  • Necessity of Municipal Licenses As per the section 417 of DMC ACT 1957(amended time to time) provides that any person whosoever desires to do trade under public health policy, is required to obtain license under Health Trade.
  • Licensees need to fulfil all the terms and condition as mentioned in Public Health Policy.
  • Applicant must ensure the availability of all documents and certificates prior to apply for the Health trade License.
  • The applicant/ Licensee cannot claim demand immunity from action or penalties on the ground that the Health Trade License has been granted without prior verification.

Hotel, Motel, Farmhouse and Other Areas Permission Application


About Us

Hotel, Motel, Farmhouse and Other Areas Permission issues by Municipal Corporation of Delhi, MCD will issue Permission to the Owner/Tenant of Hotel and Motel, Farmhouse and Other Areas approved by the Building Department. MCD-For issuance of instant Permission for Holding Social Functions by erecting the Pandals in the Open space in the premises of Hotel/Motel, Farmhouses and other areas. Provision shall be made for online signup for the first time applicants along with online submission of application, upload of supporting documents, online payments of security, permission charges (annually/half yearly as per the case) and late penalties charges as applicable. On successful submission of application, the applicant would able to download permission letter.

  • If payment is not made till cut-off date of 10th January, Rs. 10,000/- per day shall be charged for a maximum of 15 days, failing which the permission shall be treated as cancelled.

  • Area of Pandal should not be more than the total available open space as per approved/sanctioned plan and/or completion plan.
  • No change in Pandal area (declared at the time of permission) shall be allowed in a calendar year.
  • If the area of Pandal is changed at any later stage, prior permission may be obtained on applicable rates on pro-rata basis for the remaining calendar year.
  • The charges for holding social function shall be enhanced every year by 5%.
  • If any applicant filled details found to be incorrect, MCD may cancel the application.
