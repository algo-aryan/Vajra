ABOUT US

Town Planning Department has three Cells - Layout Cell, Development Project Cell and General Cell. Each Cell is headed by a under the overall control of the Chief Town Planner.

1.Layout Cell scrutinizes the layout cases under section 312-313 of the DMC Act, 1957 including the Land Use clarification and comments for reference received from various implementing agencies.

2.Development Project Cell provides civic amenities/facilities in regularization of unauthorized colonies.

3.General Cell deals with the proposals of Community facilities to be developed and located in various colonies. These facilities include Community Halls, Primary Schools, Health Centres etc.
