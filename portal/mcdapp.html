Privacy Policy

Android App Installation Guidelines

Android® 7.x & Lower

  • Go to Settings > Lock screen and security/ Security > Tap the Unknown sources switch to turn on or off.
  • Note If unavaliable, Unknown sources to turn on or off. Enabled when check mark is present.
  • To continue, review prompt then tap OK.

Android® 8.x & Higher

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Tap Menu icon (upper-right) > Tap Special access > Tap Install unknown apps.
  • Select the unknown app then tap the Allow from this source switch to turn on.

IOS App Installation Guidelines

How to download and install

  • Click on Download iOS
  • POP UP of *MCD App Install *
  • Once downloaded click on icon with name - "MCD App"
  • First and one time activity
  • *Untrusted Enterprise Developer *
  • On iOS 9.0/9.1, go to Settings > General > Profiles > tap on the developer's profile, and tap on Trust.
  • On iOS 9.2+, go to Settings > General > Device Management > tap on the developer's profile, and tap on Trust.
