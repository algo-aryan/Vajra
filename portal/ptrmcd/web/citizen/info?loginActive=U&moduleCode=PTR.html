Login Instructions For Already Registered “NRI TAXPAYERS”
  • The login process has been stopped by using international mobile numbers, therefore such NRIs/taxpayers may use any one of the following methods for login to pay their property tax seamlessly.
  • By using their Indian Mobile Number:-
    Step 1:- Sign-Up as a new user on the login page using “New User Click Here For Sign Up / Registration”
    Step 2:- Request for UPIC Transfer after Login into MCD Portal
    Step 3:- Once your request is approved by the officer, your UPIC will be bind with the new Mobile number. Now you can manage your Login by using the Indian Mobile Number & OTP.
  • By using E-mail ID:-
    Step 1:- Sign up by using the link “New User Click Here for the Sign Up / Registration” link after that select EMAIL OTP LOGIN & PASSWORD
    Step 2:- You may use your Email id as the Login ID
    Step 3:- Request for UPIC Transfer after Login into MCD Portal
    Step 4:- Once approved by the officer your request, your UPIC is available in the login id. Now you may manage your login id by using your email id and OTP received on your email id.
  • Login Instructions For New “NRI TAXPAYERS”
  • New NRI may use any of the following methods to Sign up using Sign Up / Registration link and login to pay their property tax seamlessly.
    i) By using their Indian Mobile Number
    OR
    (ii) By using the E-mail ID
